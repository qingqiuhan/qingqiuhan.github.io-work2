Introduction

Human Resources Management Tool

  • This tool is mainly used to plan and manage the company's human resources. Show the current workforce planning of the entire company in a more intuitive way

  • It is now in use

Users

  • The composition of users includes the chief managers and assistant managers of main departments, team leaders and group leaders belonged to each department


Demands

  • View and edit the organizational structure of the organization (Departments, teams and group)

  • Manage allocation of human resources

  • Aggregating information on insufficient human resources

Further analysis

  • There is ONLY ONE type of architecture information for ONE organization.

  • Users could not edit two organizations simultaneously.

  • For users who are responsible for different departments at the same time, this tool should provide an entrance to let users switch managed organizations

  • When editing, users could not use three export buttons, for these statistics are associated with the content of edit area.

Feedback

After I finished my internship in this company. I wanted to know whether this tool work effectively and I queried my colleagues for users feedback. There are two main problems.

Problem 1

  • The area of personnel management is too small to be easy to operate. Especially for organizations with more than 20 employees

Problem 2

  • Edit buttion and three export buttons obstruct some part of edit area. It is a little troulbe.

Solution (personal suggestions)

Explanation

  • Put edit button to the left side, expand Personnel Management area, and they and Module make up the left area. They would be used when user editing.

  • Put Organization Management and three export buttons to the right side. They make up the right area and they are all associated with the content of edit area. Besides, Organization Management become closer to the drop-down list(used to switch managed organization).

  • Users could fold and unfold the right area. There would be a bigger edit area.
  • When editing, the save button would appear on the right, the cancel button would appear on the left. It would be more convenient for users to save quickly.

  • Because the distance between three export buttons and save/cancel button is relativly large. When users editing, three export buttons would be disable, and a promopt would show up.
